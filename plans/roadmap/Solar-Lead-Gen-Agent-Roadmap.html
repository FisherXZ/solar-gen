My Vision

The problem and what we're building to solve it.

The Problem

Manual Lead Discovery Is Broken

Today
  • Sales finds leads through trade shows & word of mouth
  • Projects slip through — competitors get there first
  • No way to know WHO is building a project
  • Every lead in the database is epc_company: NULL
With This Tool
  • Continuously discovers upcoming solar projects
  • Identifies the EPC contractor — the actual buyer
  • Delivers actionable intelligence to sales
  • Leads arrive with the EPC name attached

"A lead with a known EPC is a sales conversation. A lead with better coordinates is a prettier map pin."

Data Sources

Project Lifecycle: When Each Source Appears

Cross-referencing multiple sources reveals how far along a project is — a project in only the ISO queue has a 60-80% withdrawal rate. One also in EIA-860 with a FERC LGIA is almost certainly going to construction.

3–5 yrs out
ISO Queue Entry
Queue ID, county/state, MW, developer. Earliest public record of a project.
2–3 yrs out
FERC LGIA Filing
Interconnection agreement signed. Project is committed. Legal entity confirmed.
1–2 yrs out
EIA Form 860
Exact lat/lng, EIA plant ID, owner info, planned COD. Government-mandated filing.
0–1 yr out
EIA 860M + News
Construction month, actual COD. Press releases name the EPC contractor.
Operational
USPVDB
As-built capacity, site polygon. Validation and knowledge graph enrichment.

The difference between "there's a solar project in Texas" and "EPC Y is about to break ground on a 250MW project at these coordinates in June 2027."

EPC Discovery

The 5 EPC Disclosure Channels

No single registry maps projects to their EPC contractors. They surface through 5 different channels with different coverage, reliability, and timing.

Channel Coverage Reliability Timing
Developer Press Releases 60–70% of >100MW High — first-party Days after financial close
Trade Publications 50–60% of >100MW High — named sources Days to weeks
EPC Company Websites 30–40% High — self-reported Variable (weeks–months)
SEC / Financial Filings 10–15% Very High — mandated Days to quarters
State Utility Filings 10–15% High — regulatory Weeks to months

Verified

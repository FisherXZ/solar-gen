## 3. Oberon Solar Portfolio — CONTRADICTED

**Claimed:** 728MW · Intersect Power → Signal Energy · Source: Developer PR

### Verdict

**The claim conflates two separate Intersect Power announcements.** The 728MW + Signal Energy combination is real — but it refers to the Radian + Athos III projects, NOT Oberon. Oberon's EPC was likely SOLV Energy.

### What Actually Happened

| What was claimed | What the 728MW actually refers to | Oberon's actual details |
|------------------|-----------------------------------|------------------------|
| Oberon Solar, 728MW, Signal Energy EPC | **Radian (415MW, TX) + Athos III (313MW, CA)** — announced July 2021 with Signal Energy as EPC | **679 MWp / 500 MWac** solar + 250MW/1GWh storage, Riverside County CA, EPC likely **SOLV Energy** |

### Sources — The 728MW / Signal Energy Announcement (Radian + Athos III, NOT Oberon)

1. **Intersect Power PR (July 20, 2021)** — This is where the 728MW figure comes from
   - https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/intersect-power-selects-signal-energy-and-nextracker-for-two-utility-scale-solar-projects-in-texas-and-california-301337376.html
   - *"Intersect Power selected Signal Energy as EPC for the 415MW Radian project (Texas) and the 313MW Athos III project (California)."*

2. **Solar Power World (July 2021)**
   - https://www.solarpowerworldonline.com/2021/07/intersect-power-taps-signal-energy-nextracker-texas-california-solar/

3. **PV Tech (July 2021)**
   - https://www.pv-tech.org/us-round-up-signal-energy-nextracker-selected-for-728mw-us-portfolio-longroad-energy-seals-financing-for-200mwdc/

### Sources — Oberon's Actual EPC (SOLV Energy)

4. **SOLV Energy Project Page — Oberon 1**
   - https://www.solvenergy.com/projects/oberon-1
   - Lists Oberon 1 (339 MWdc) as a SOLV Energy EPC project

5. **Electrical Consultants Inc. (ECI)**
   - https://eciusa.com/oberon-solar-project/
   - *"ECI partnered with SOLV Energy on the Oberon Solar Project."*

6. **Intersect Power — Oberon COD Announcement (November 2023)**
   - https://www.intersect.com/news/intersect-power-reaches-commercial-operation-of-oberon-solar-storage-project
   - Confirms 679 MWp / 500 MWac solar + 250MW/1GWh storage. No mention of Signal Energy.

### Reasoning

The error likely occurred because a September 2019 Signal Energy press release announced a 5-project, 1.7GW portfolio with Intersect Power that included Oberon I and Oberon II. However, Signal Energy was EPC on only "three of the five" projects — and by the time Oberon entered construction, SOLV Energy appears as the EPC on their project page. The 728MW figure was then mistakenly attributed to Oberon rather than its actual source (the July 2021 Radian + Athos III announcement).

### Corrected Entry

The correct entry should be one of:
- **Radian Solar, 415MW, Intersect Power → Signal Energy** (confirmed, TX)
- **Athos III Solar, 313MW, Intersect Power → Signal Energy** (confirmed, CA)
- **Oberon Solar, 679 MWp, Intersect Power → SOLV Energy** (likely, CA)
